Me In A Nutshell

I was born at Jefferson Hospital, December 20th, 1991. Growing up in Northeast Philadelphia, I developed a love for Philadelphia sports and the local music scene while in grade school and high school. Unsure of what career path I would take in life, I became interested in learning how to code and following web development as a career. After hearing about the New York Code and Design Academy through my sister, I decided to apply to the Web Development Intensive course which began on November 30th, 2015.
